Perfumes

The project was designed to express two distinct olfactory worlds through visual contrasts and emotional depth. Centered around the themes of fire and frost, the campaign explores how scent can be translated into powerful digital imagery that speaks to mood and identity.

Sauvage was brought to life using a cold, stony aesthetic — sharp textures, slate grays, and icy blues form a wintery, almost elemental atmosphere. It feels distant, bold, and untamed. Fahrenheit, on the other hand, radiates warmth: soft gradients of crimson and amber flow like heat waves, with fluid lines that echo movement and passion.

By presenting these opposing moods side by side, the campaign balances tension and harmony. The visuals are not just representations of fragrance, but immersive portraits of character. Together, they tell a story of contrast — of cold clarity and burning intensity — designed to resonate with a modern, style-conscious audience.
