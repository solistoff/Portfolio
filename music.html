In the first work, emotional fragility is conveyed with a soft cloud in the shape of a soap bubble against a bold red background. The contrast between the delicate bubble and intense red creates a quiet tension, symbolizing vulnerability and strength.

In the second work, a green Adidas sneaker is the focal point, surrounded by hand-drawn “Adidas” typography in the background. The contrast between the clean sneaker and textured lettering brings energy and movement, visualizing brand culture with an artistic touch. The design blends modern streetwear with creative expression, capturing the essence of the brand’s identity.

In the third work, a metal chain stretches across an industrial backdrop, symbolizing restraint and tension. The stark lighting and dark textures enhance the harsh, controlling energy of the visual, mirroring the aggressive tone of the track.
